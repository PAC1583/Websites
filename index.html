Climate Change

Climate Change

Emission of greenhouse gases, such as methane and carbon dioxide, has contributed to the increase of the earth's temperature from the mid-20th century. The rise of sea level due to the melting of glaciers in polar regions, more frequent floodings or less predictable weather patterns are some of the consequenses of this problematic.

One of my missions to fight against climate change is to make small contributions such as: reduce the use of a car or airplane. Instead use the train, bus or walk if possible. Try to buy local food and avoid as much as possible the consumption of meats and processed food.


Sustanaibility

Sustainability

Sustainabitily means "meeting the needs of the present generation without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs". In other words, natural resources are limited and their use should as well be limited or find alternative raw materials that exists in abundance on earth. In this way, the present generation will assure that next generations will also have the chance to have access to all what we enjoy nowadays!
In order to shift to a more sustainable economy, goverments need to set more stringent laws and corporations should modify their manufacturing processes. Form my end, I can also play a role by applying what is called 3R's: reuse, recycle and reduce. This implies to limit the use plastic materials or packings, recycle materials such as aluminium cans or plastic bottles and avoid buying what is not neccesary.
